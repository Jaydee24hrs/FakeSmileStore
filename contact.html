Quick Answers

Frequently asked.

How do drops work?

Each drop is a small, limited run. Once it's gone, it's gone. Members on the list get early access, full restock notifications and members-only colorways.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes — we currently ship to 12+ countries direct from Lagos. Orders over ₦100,000 ship free domestically; international rates calculated at checkout.

What's your return policy?

14-day exchanges on unworn pieces with tags attached. Drop us a line at hello@fakesmile.brand and we'll start the process the same day.

Wholesale & collabs?

Always open. Hit press@fakesmile.brand with your stockist or collaboration brief and the team will reach back within 48 hours.
