The Origin

Wear confidence, even when the smile is fake.

FakeSmile started in 2024 with a single sketchbook, a brick wall, and one conviction — that fashion should speak when silence won't. Every drop is engineered for motion, identity, and the streets we came from.

From Lagos workshops to closets across continents, the FakeSmile crew is building a movement, not a moment. Limited runs. Heavyweight cotton. Signature stitching. No shortcuts.
