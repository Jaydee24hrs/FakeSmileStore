SUMMARY
--------------------------------------------------------------
Visually and front-end-wise, the site is roughly 90% there — it
looks and behaves like a real premium store (8 pages, 43 products,
cart, checkout, orders, currency, promo codes).

But functionally it is still a SHOWCASE, not a SHOP — because a
customer can complete the entire flow and the seller would never
know they ordered. The items below close that gap.


==============================================================
  RED — CRITICAL (can't actually run a store without these)
==============================================================

1. NO BACKEND / ORDERS GO NOWHERE      [DONE]
   Cloudflare Worker (worker.js) proxies payment + verification
   with Nomba. EmailJS sends a seller alert to your inbox every
   time an order is placed. See DEPLOY-WORKER.md for setup.

2. PAYMENT IS A STUB                   [DONE]
   Wired to Nomba (Nigerian payment processor). Checkout now
   shows "Pay with Nomba" -> redirects to Nomba's hosted page
   for card / USSD / bank transfer -> verifies on return.
   "Bank transfer (manual)" remains as a fallback option.

3. ORDER CONFIRMATION EMAIL IS FAKE    [DONE]
   EmailJS now fires TWO emails after every paid order:
     - Seller alert (to you) with full order details
     - Customer confirmation (to the buyer)
   Free tier covers ~100 orders/month.

4. CONTACT & NEWSLETTER FORMS DON'T SUBMIT
   STILL OPEN. Easy fix later: same EmailJS account, different
   templates. ~15 min of work once Nomba+EmailJS is live.
